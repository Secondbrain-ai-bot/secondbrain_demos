Methodology Note
How OTIF Exposure is calculated:
For each at-risk order: Exposure = Product Revenue Rate ($/mt) × At-Risk Quantity (mt)

Revenue rates used: Benzene $9,000/mt · Toluene $7,500/mt · Xylene $8,500/mt · Aromatics $6,000/mt
These represent the revenue opportunity at risk — i.e., what the company stands to lose in fulfilled revenue if the dispatch fails OTIF. Naphtha (raw material) is excluded as it is not a direct dispatch commitment. Exposure resets to zero if the order is fulfilled on time or an alternate is found.
